This Privacy Policy describes how the LeakDetection mobile application ("App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our leak detection and reporting service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

We collect your device's location data to:

  • Enable accurate leak location reporting
  • Find nearest water meters in your vicinity
  • Track field worker positions for service coordination
Note: Location data is only collected when you are actively using the app and have granted location permissions.

1.2 Camera and Photos

We access your device camera to:

  • Capture photos of water leaks for documentation
  • Attach visual evidence to leak reports

Photos are only captured when you explicitly take them within the app.

1.3 Account Information

When you log in, we collect:

  • Username/Employee ID
  • Authentication credentials (securely stored)

1.4 Leak Report Data

Information you provide in leak reports:

  • Leak descriptions and classifications
  • Customer/meter information
  • Photos and location coordinates
  • Timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and manage leak reports
  • Coordinate field service operations
  • Improve water infrastructure maintenance
  • Generate reports and analytics for service improvement
  • Authenticate users and secure the application

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored on your device
  • Offline data is stored locally and synced when connectivity is restored
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with:

  • Water utility management systems for leak processing
  • Authorized supervisors and field coordinators
  • Third-party services required for app functionality (e.g., mapping services)

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Maintain records for audit purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for data collection
  • Disable location or camera permissions in your device settings

7. Offline Functionality

The app stores data locally on your device for offline use. This includes:

  • Customer and meter information for offline search
  • Draft reports pending submission
  • Cached map tiles for offline navigation

This data remains on your device until synced with our servers or manually cleared.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for authorized personnel only and is not designed for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or other appropriate means.
